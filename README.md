## Design & Interactivity
Both dashboards were designed with usability and executive consumption in mind.

**Interactivity includes:**
- Dynamic year selection for historical analysis

- Easy navigation between Sales and Customer dashboards

**Filters available:**
- Product Category
- Product Subcategory
- Region
- State
- City
